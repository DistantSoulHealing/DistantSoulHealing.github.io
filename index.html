About Me

    

Hi, I’m Andrea Montgomery, and I specialize in intuitive and soul readings as well as healing techniques for both humans and pets. My journey has led me to explore the deeper connections between mind, body, and soul, helping individuals uncover what’s holding them back from living their best lives. Through practices like Bodytalk, Akashic Record readings, and Emotion Code sessions, I aim to empower you to rediscover your inner strength and find clarity on your path.

    

Services

    

Bodytalk

    

Chronic stress can create breakdowns in communication between the mind and body. Bodytalk identifies these breakdowns using biofeedback (muscle checking), bringing to awareness what needs to be addressed first. Using tapping techniques and concepts from both eastern and western medicine, Bodytalk helps re-establish communication and balance, allowing your body’s own healing capabilities to be fully activated.

    

Session Duration: 45-60 minutes (in-person or distance sessions available).

    

Learn more about Bodytalk @ IBA Global Healing | Bodytalk

    

Akashic Record Readings

    

The Akashic Records are a spiritual repository of every soul's past, present, and future possibilities. During an Akashic Record reading, I access this sacred information to provide insight, guidance, and healing tailored to your unique soul journey. These readings can help you uncover your soul’s purpose, release past-life blockages, and gain clarity on your spiritual path.

    

Intuitive Readings

    

In an intuitive reading, I connect with your energy and spirit guides to offer insight and clarity regarding different areas of your life. These readings provide guidance on relationships, career, health, and personal growth, helping you navigate life’s challenges with a sense of empowerment and peace.

    

Distant Reiki & Energy Work

    

Reiki is a form of energy healing that promotes relaxation, reduces stress, and stimulates the body’s natural healing processes. Through distant Reiki sessions, I work with universal energy to help balance your energy field and clear blockages that may be causing physical or emotional distress, no matter where you are located.

    

Emotion Code

    

The Emotion Code is a healing method designed to identify and release trapped emotions that may be holding you back in life. These emotional blockages can manifest physically and emotionally, and the Emotion Code aims to clear these imbalances.
